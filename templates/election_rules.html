Election Rules & Guidelines

1. Eligibility

  • Only registered students of DUT are allowed to vote.
  • Each voter can cast only one vote per position.
  • Candidates must be approved by the admin before running for office.

2. Voting Process

  • Voting will be conducted online through the official platform.
  • Each voter must log in using their student ID and email.
  • Once a vote is cast, it cannot be changed or revoked.

3. Candidate Guidelines

  • Candidates must be members of one of the registered student movements (EFFSC, SASCO, MK Students Movement).
  • Campaigning must adhere to university guidelines and remain respectful.
  • False information or bribery will lead to disqualification.

4. Election Results

  • Results will be announced after verification.
  • Any disputes must be submitted to the admin within 24 hours.
  • The candidate with the most votes wins the position.

5. General Conduct

  • Voters and candidates must respect election integrity.
  • Any attempt to manipulate or hack the system will result in disciplinary action.
